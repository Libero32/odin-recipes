Cevapi

Description

Ćevapi has its origins in the Balkans during the Ottoman period, and represents a regional speciality similar to the köfte kebab. They are usually served in groups of five to ten pieces on a plate or in a flatbread (lepinja or somun), often with chopped onions, sour cream, kajmak (milk cream), ajvar (relish), and salt.

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg junetine
  • 0.5kg janjetine
  • 4 čena luka
  • 2 kafene kasike sode bikarbone

Steps

  1. Meso izrezati na kocke 2x2cm i posoliti
  2. Prokuhati luk
  3. Meso pustiti da prenoci
  4. Samljeti meso
  5. Oblikovati meso u cevape
  6. Ispeci na rostilju
